Building for the Future

RSP Permian is now a part of Concho. Today we are the largest unconventional shale producer in the Permian Basin, working together to deliver strong performance and shareholder returns. We are powered by a great team, a leading portfolio of opportunities and a strong financial position.

Pure Permian

Concho pursues oil and gas exploration and development exclusively in this part of Texas and New Mexico, one of the most prolific producing oil and gas regions in the U.S. Our deep knowledge of the area enables us to seek high-quality assets while capitalizing on the strategic benefits of being one of the largest operators in the region.
